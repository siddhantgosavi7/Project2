Ekalavy Weather App Project

Welcome to the Ekalavy project! This application is designed to provide you with **simple, fast, and accurate weather information** in a modern, user-friendly interface. Our goal is to make checking the weather a clean and straightforward experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to essential environmental data. By focusing on a minimalist design and core functionality, we aim to deliver forecasts without the clutter and complexity often found in other weather applications.

Key Features

  • Real-time Temperature
  • Humidity & Wind Speed
  • Clean, Minimalist Design
  • Secure User Authentication

Get Started

To access the full weather forecasting features, please **Log In** or **Sign Up** using the links in the navigation bar. Your journey to simple weather tracking starts here!
